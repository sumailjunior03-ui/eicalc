Contact EICalc.ca

Questions about the calculator, data issues, or partnership inquiries.

Not Service Canada. EICalc.ca cannot access your EI claim, check your application status, or make eligibility decisions. For official EI support, call Service Canada at 1-800-206-7218 or visit canada.ca/ei.

What We Can Help With

  • Questions about how the calculator works
  • Why a city maps to a particular EI region
  • Suspected data errors (regional rate appears stale, calculation seems wrong)
  • Feedback on usability or missing cities
  • Partnership and advertising inquiries

What We Can't Help With

  • Official claim status or payment inquiries
  • ROE disputes or missing records of employment
  • Appeals or reconsideration requests
  • Decisions made by Service Canada
  • Individual eligibility determinations

For all of the above, contact Service Canada directly at 1-800-206-7218 or visit a Service Canada Centre.
