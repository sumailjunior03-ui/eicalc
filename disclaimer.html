Important limitations and disclosures about EICalc.ca.

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Estimation Only

All results produced by EICalc.ca are estimates. This calculator applies the publicly available Employment Insurance rates published by Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC). Results are intended for informational and planning purposes only. They should not be used as official payroll deduction amounts, submitted on tax forms, or treated as authoritative EI figures.

Not an Official Government Tool

EICalc.ca is an independent website. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Government of Canada, Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC), or Service Canada. For official EI deduction amounts and eligibility information, consult your employer's payroll department or visit canada.ca.

Situations This Calculator May Not Accurately Model

The following circumstances may produce results that differ from your actual EI deductions:

Quebec residents pay a reduced EI premium rate due to the Quebec Parental Insurance Plan (QPIP). This calculator uses the standard non-Quebec rate and does not apply to Quebec employees.

Self-employed individuals who have voluntarily opted into EI for special benefits are subject to different rate structures not modelled here.

Employees with multiple employers in a single year may over-contribute — this calculator does not account for combined deductions across employers.

Partial-year employment, mid-year employment changes, or income that is only partially insurable may produce differences from this calculator's output.

Insurable earnings rules under the Employment Insurance Act may exclude certain types of income from the EI base. This calculator assumes all entered income is fully insurable.

Rate Currency

EI rates and the Maximum Insurable Earnings are set annually by ESDC. While we update this calculator when new rates are announced, there may be a period where the calculator reflects rates from the prior year. Always verify current rates at canada.ca before relying on this tool for important financial decisions.
