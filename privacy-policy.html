Privacy Policy

What we collect, what we don't, and how this site works.

Last updated: March 30, 2026

The Short Version

EICalc.ca does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial information you enter into the calculator. All calculations run entirely in your browser. Your income data never leaves your device.

Calculator Data

When you enter an income amount into the EI Calculator, that number is processed locally by JavaScript running in your browser. It is not sent to any server, not stored in any database, and not visible to us in any form. Closing or refreshing the page clears all entered data.

Analytics

EICalc.ca may use standard web analytics to understand aggregate usage — such as page view counts and general traffic trends. Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated; it does not identify individual users or their income inputs. We do not track individuals across sessions.

Advertising

EICalc.ca may display advertisements through Google AdSense (publisher ID: ca-pub-7744853829365165). Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads relevant to your interests. These cookies are managed by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy, not by EICalc.ca. You can review Google's advertising privacy controls at google.com/settings/ads.

Cookies

EICalc.ca does not set first-party cookies for tracking. Third-party services on the site (such as Google AdSense) may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies.

Links to Other Sites

EICalc.ca contains links to other calculator sites in the Canadian network (such as calc-hq.ca, cppcalc.ca, ontariotakehomecalc.ca). These are independent sites with their own privacy practices. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at partnerships@calc-hq.ca. Since EICalc.ca does not collect personal information through the calculator, in most cases there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
