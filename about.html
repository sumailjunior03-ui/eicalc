About

A free, independent Employment Insurance contribution estimator for Canadian employees.

What Is EICalc.ca?

EICalc.ca is a free, browser-based Employment Insurance (EI) contribution estimator designed for Canadian employees. It allows anyone to quickly estimate their annual EI deductions based on their gross insurable earnings, using the current year's officially published ESDC rates and Maximum Insurable Earnings.

This tool was built because understanding your EI deductions shouldn't require a payroll reference guide. The calculation is simple — but the terminology and the annual cap behaviour are often unclear. EICalc.ca aims to eliminate that confusion with a transparent, well-explained tool.

⚠️ EICalc.ca is an independent estimation tool. It is not affiliated with the Government of Canada, Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC), or Service Canada. All results are estimates only and should not be used as official filing amounts.

Who Built This?

EICalc.ca is part of a Canadian financial utility network built by independent web publishers dedicated to creating clear, free, and accurate financial reference tools for Canadians. The network is connected through calc-hq.ca, which serves as the central hub and authority resource for the Canadian calculator cluster.

Related tools in the network include a CPP calculator and an Ontario take-home pay calculator — all built on the same principles of accuracy, transparency, and browser-only operation. No data ever leaves your device.

All tools are built with static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No backend, no databases, no user accounts.

Our Approach to Accuracy

All EI rates, the Maximum Insurable Earnings, and the employer multiplier used in this calculator are sourced from publicly available Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC) publications. We review and update parameters annually when the federal government announces changes.

2026 EI Parameters Used

  • Maximum Insurable Earnings (MIE): $68,900
  • Employee Premium Rate: 1.63%
  • Maximum Annual Employee Contribution: $1,123.07
  • Employer Multiplier: 1.4× employee premium
  • Maximum Annual Employer Contribution: $1,572.30

While we strive for accuracy, this is not an official ESDC tool. Results should be treated as estimates. Payroll calculations for specific pay periods may differ slightly due to per-period rounding or employment circumstances not modelled here.

Privacy and Data

We are committed to your privacy. EICalc.ca does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal or financial information you enter. All calculations run entirely within your browser — no data is sent anywhere.

The site uses standard analytics to understand aggregate usage (page visits and traffic trends), but no personally identifiable information is ever collected. For full details, read our Privacy Policy.

Limitations and Scope

EICalc.ca is designed as a planning and educational tool. It does not model every employment situation. Key limitations:

  • Quebec employees pay a reduced EI rate due to the Quebec Parental Insurance Plan (QPIP). This calculator uses the standard non-Quebec rate.
  • Self-employed individuals who have opted into EI for special benefits have different rates and conditions — not modelled here.
  • Partial-year employment, multiple employers, or non-standard insurable income may produce differences from actual deductions.
  • EI eligibility for benefits is not calculated here — only the premium contribution amount.

For questions about your specific situation, consult your employer's payroll team or Service Canada at canada.ca.
